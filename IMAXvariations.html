IMAX Variations

  • IMAX Dome/OMNIMAX
    • Domed screen with special lens to fill entire field of vision
    • More immersive experience
    • Uses analog format
  • IMAX 3D
    • Dual projectors
    • Curved screen
    • Uncompressed audio
  • IMAX HD
    • 48 fps
  • Digital IMAX
    • Lower maximum perceived resolution (2.9k vs. 12k) compared to traditional
  • IMAXShift
    • Indoor cycling concept with a large screen
