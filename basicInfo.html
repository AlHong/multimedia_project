Basic Information

  • IMAX: acronym for "image maximum"
  • Created by Graeme Ferguson, Roman Kroitor, Robert Kerr, and William Shaw
  • Is a motion picture film format and cinema projection standard
  • Can record and display images of greater size and resolution than standard film systems
  • is the most used system for special venue film presentations
